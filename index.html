About Kenny Ray's Place

At Kenny Ray’s Place, you aren’t a number, you are a friend. Ken believes that your haircut shouldn’t be a rush job but a piece of work that requires care and precision. Ken takes his time and gets to know you and your style. He has 9 years of experience as a stylist and takes pride in each cut that he delivers to his clients. You will notice the difference from the moment you walk into the shop. His passion for the craft and his love for people will make you feel right at home.

“A great haircut should make you feel like the best version of yourself. This is what I love to do, and I am committed to providing each client with precisely that experience.”

Kenneth Wells, Stylist
